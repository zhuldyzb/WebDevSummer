Turkey Taco Bowls

  • Time: 13 min
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
ground turkey 1 pound
envelope taco seasoning mix 2 tablespoons
package precooked microwavable white rice, heated according to package directions 2

Step 1
Heat the oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add the turkey and cook, stirring often with a wooden spoon to crumble the meat into smaller pieces, until cooked through, about 5 minutes.

Step 2
Divide the rice and optional shredded lettuce evenly among four bowls. Top evenly with turkey mixture.

Step 3
Add the taco seasoning, water, and salt; cook, stirring constantly, until meat is coated in the seasoning, about 1 minute.

Kimchi and Spam Fried Rice

  • Time: 30 mins
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Kimchi 1.5 cups
Spam 6 ounces
Vegetables 1, 1

Step 1
In a large saucepan over a medium-low heat, warm the oil. Add onion and garlic for 3 to 5 minutes. Crisp the Spam.

Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 1.5 cups
Baking powder 3.5 teaspoons
Olive oil 2 tablespoon
Milk 1.25 cups
Egg 1 piece

Step 1
In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

Step 2
Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
